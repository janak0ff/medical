Orthopaedics

  1. Category

Orthopaedics is a medical specialty focused on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of musculoskeletal disorders and injuries. Learn about the common conditions treated by orthopaedic doctors, such as arthritis, fractures, and joint pain, and the various treatment options available, including surgery, physical therapy, and medications.
This presentation helps in learning the anatomy of the joint bones, joint health and the changes that take place in the joint tissues and bones when they are affected by osteoarthritis. Apart from the risk factors and causes, various treatment options and ways to cope with the disease are also dealt with.
